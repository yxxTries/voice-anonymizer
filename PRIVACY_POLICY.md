# Privacy Policy

Voice Anonymizer processes microphone audio locally in your browser to transform the sound of your voice in real time.

## Data collection

- The extension does not upload microphone audio to our servers.
- The extension does not sell personal data.
- The extension does not require an account.
- The extension stores your selected settings locally in Chrome storage so your preferences can persist between sessions.

## Audio processing

- Audio is processed on-device inside the browser.
- The extension injects an audio processing script into pages where microphone input is used.
- The processed audio is used to anonymize the voice before it is passed to web applications.

## Permissions

- `storage`: used to save your local extension settings.
- Site access on pages where the extension runs: needed so the microphone stream can be anonymized in the page context.

## Third parties

- The extension does not send audio or settings to third-party services.

## Changes to this policy

- If the extension behavior changes in a way that affects data handling, this policy should be updated before release.
