## Extension name

Voice Anonymizer

## Single purpose statement

Transforms live microphone audio in the browser to anonymize voice identity while preserving speech clarity.

## Short description (<=132 chars)

Anonymize your microphone voice in real time with local, on-device processing and adjustable voice style controls.

## Detailed description

Voice Anonymizer helps you mask voice identity during live microphone use while keeping speech clear and understandable.

What it does:
- Intercepts microphone audio in pages where mic input is used.
- Applies real-time voice anonymization effects.
- Lets you choose voice style presets or tune advanced parameters.
- Includes a preview page to compare results before use.

Privacy-first behavior:
- Audio processing runs locally in your browser.
- No account is required.
- Microphone audio is not uploaded to our servers.
- Settings are stored locally in Chrome storage.

Best for:
- Privacy-aware calls and live communication.
- Reducing voice-identifying characteristics while preserving intelligibility.
